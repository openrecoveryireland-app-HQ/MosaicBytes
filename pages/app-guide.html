Mosaic Guide

This page will explain the main parts of Mosaic and how each feature can be used in your own way.

What This Guide Will Cover

Mosaic is being built as a private personal support app. The guide will explain the main features clearly, including what each part does and how it can help.

  • New days counter.
  • Hold the Line support tools.
  • Private journal.
  • Reflections and saved quotes.
  • Meeting and support links.
  • Reminders and personal tools.
  • Games and distraction tools.
